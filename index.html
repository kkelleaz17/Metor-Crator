History

About 50,000 years ago during the Pleistocene epoch, a 30 to 50 meter asteroid hit earth. The exploding with a force 150 times greater than the atomic bomb! The result was 175 million tons of rock, forming a crater nearly a mile wide and 570 feet deep.Because of the crater's young age and the dry climate Meteor Crater is the best preserved impact crater on Earth. But sadly most of the asteroid vaporized upon impact, leaving few remains in the crater.
